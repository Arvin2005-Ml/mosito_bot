About Us

Pioneering the Education of Modern Technologies

Mositto Academy was founded with the aim of empowering the new generation of specialists and enthusiasts of advanced technologies. We believe the future is built by those who master modern tools. Therefore, we offer the most comprehensive and up-to-date courses in AI, Robotics, and Programming, utilizing experienced instructors and modern teaching methods.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and the real needs of the industry. At Mositto, students not only learn theoretical concepts but also get ready for the global job market by working on practical, real-world projects.
